About the App:
StockHawk is a mobile guide to current and historical stock quote data. It provides the following features:

  • The creation of a customized list of stocks.
  • Existing list entries can be deleted by simply swiping them the the side.
  • New list entries can be added by simply clicking on the button in the lower right corner.
  • Automated updates of current stock details every fifteen minutes.
  • The ability to view individual stock history over the past two years on a monthly basis. The history details include the opening, closing, high, and low values.
  • A widget which cycles through the list of stocks. It can also be manually cycled through, in either direction, or a particular stock can be clicked which displays the historical graph.
